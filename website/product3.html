Product Details

The Swiss Cheese Plant, also known as Monstera deliciosa, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America. It is prized for its large, glossy leaves with distinctive splits and perforations, giving them a unique and eye-catching appearance. Swiss Cheese Plants thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making them popular choices for indoor gardens and tropical-themed decor.
